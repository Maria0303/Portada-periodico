The bogotazo

On April 9, 1948, the liberal caudillo Jorge Eliecer Gaitán was in his office with Plinio Mendoza Neira, Pedro Eliseo Cruz, Alejandro Vallejo and Jorge Padilla. They headed for lunch at 1:00 p.m. and, when they got out of the elevator, Mendoza Neira took Gaitán by the arm and they went ahead of the rest of the people.

Juan Roa Sierra, upon reaching the door, apparently shoots at the politician. Three bullets hit the body of Gaitán, who died a few minutes later in the Central Clinic while his friend, the doctor Pedro Eliseo Cruz, proceeded to perform a blood transfusion.
